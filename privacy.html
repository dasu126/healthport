Privacy Policy for HealthPort（健康港隐私政策）

Overview / 概述

HealthPort respects your privacy. HealthPort does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

HealthPort 非常重视您的隐私。本应用不会收集、存储或上传任何个人数据到外部服务器。

HealthKit Data / HealthKit 数据访问

HealthPort accesses Apple HealthKit data only with your permission. The app uses this data solely to export structured files for your personal use.

HealthPort 仅在获得您的授权后访问 Apple HealthKit 数据，并仅用于导出结构化文件供用户个人使用。

All HealthKit data processing happens locally on your device. No health data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared externally.

所有 HealthKit 数据处理均在设备本地完成，不会上传或共享至任何服务器。

Analytics / 数据分析

HealthPort does not use analytics tools or tracking technologies.

HealthPort 不使用任何统计分析工具或用户追踪技术。

Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

HealthPort does not include third-party advertising SDKs or tracking services.

HealthPort 不集成任何广告 SDK 或第三方追踪服务。

Data Control / 数据控制权

You remain fully in control of your health data at all times.

您的健康数据始终完全由您自己控制。
