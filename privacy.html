Privacy Policy for HealthPort

HealthPort respects your privacy.

HealthPort does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

HealthKit Data

HealthPort accesses Apple HealthKit data only with your permission. The app uses this data solely to export structured files for your personal use.

All HealthKit data processing happens locally on your device. No health data is uploaded, shared, or transmitted outside your device.

Analytics

HealthPort does not use analytics tools or tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

HealthPort does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or tracking services.

Data Control

You remain fully in control of your health data at all times. Exported files are stored only in locations you choose.
